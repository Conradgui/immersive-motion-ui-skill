RRespiraTrace
Respiratory triage for small clinics

Risk signals clinicians can read before the waiting room backs up.

RespiraTrace helps clinic teams organize respiratory infection risk from intake notes, symptom windows, and point-of-care test context. It supports triage decisions; it does not replace clinician review.

No diagnostic accuracy, clearance, or treatment claim is made in this demo surface.
